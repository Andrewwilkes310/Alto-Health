Senior Care Clinics

Medicare Advantage primary care, built around you.

Our neighborhood clinics deliver the kind of care that's hard to find anymore — long visits, doctors who know your name, and a team that coordinates everything from your medications to your specialist appointments. Alto serves Medicare Advantage members exclusively, which means we can spend more time on what matters: keeping you healthy.

  • Primary care visits with your dedicated physician
  • Chronic condition management (diabetes, heart, kidney)
  • Preventive screenings and annual wellness visits
  • On-call provider access
  • Telehealth visits
  • Care coordination across specialists and pharmacies

Alto MSO

Operational backbone for primary care and specialty practices.

Alto's Management Services Organization gives independent physician groups, IPAs, and specialty practices the infrastructure to thrive — without giving up clinical autonomy. Whether you're a solo PCP looking for back-office support or a group navigating value-based contracting, we partner with you to take the operational weight off the practice.

  • Revenue cycle: billing and coding
  • Clinical administration
  • Payor contracting and network strategy
