Senior primary care, the way it should be.

Built around long visits, real relationships, and a team that knows your name.

Alto Health was built on a simple idea: seniors deserve doctors who actually have time for them.

We're a South Florida primary care practice serving Medicare Advantage members across our growing network of neighborhood clinics. We believe in spending more time with fewer patients, coordinating every piece of care, and treating each member like family.

Behind our clinics is the Alto MSO — the operational platform that lets our physicians focus on patients while we handle billing, coding, contracting, and clinical administration. It's the same model that allows the best primary care groups in the country to deliver exceptional care without the burnout.

Two locations today. More on the way.

What we believe

Most primary care visits in America last about 20 minutes. That's not enough time for a real conversation, let alone a careful clinical decision. We do it differently — longer visits, smaller panels, and a clinical team that actually knows you and the medications you're on.

Who we serve

Alto is exclusively focused on Medicare Advantage members. By specializing, we can deliver care that meets the needs of seniors managing chronic conditions, multiple medications, and the complexity that comes with aging well.

Family approach

Patients aren't numbers. We treat every member like one of our own.

Whole-person care

We treat the person, not the chart. Mind, body, and what's going on at home.

Local roots

Built in South Florida, for South Florida. Multi-lingual care that fits the community.
